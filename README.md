# 🚀 Customer Purchase Behavior & Revenue Optimization Analysis

---

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to uncover patterns in purchasing, customer segmentation, and revenue generation.

The goal is to help businesses improve **customer targeting, retention, and revenue optimization** using data-driven insights.

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to understand:

* Which customers generate the most revenue
* What drives repeat purchases
* Whether discounts and promotions are effective
* How customer segments behave differently

Without these insights, companies risk losing revenue and inefficiently allocating marketing efforts.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify high-value and low-value customer segments
* Analyze purchasing behavior across categories
* Evaluate customer frequency and average spend
* Understand customer conversion and retention
* Provide actionable strategies for revenue growth

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL Server (SSMS)** – Data extraction
* **Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)** – Data analysis
* **Power BI** – Dashboard & visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Customer shopping behavior dataset
* ~3,900 customers analyzed
* Includes:

  * Demographics (age, gender)
  * Purchase data (category, amount)
  * Customer behavior (frequency, repeat purchases)
  * Marketing data (discounts, promo codes)
  * Ratings and preferences

## 🔍 Key Analysis

### 📊 Customer Segmentation

* Customers categorized into **high, medium, and low value**
* High-value customers contribute significantly to revenue

---

### 📊 Purchase Behavior

* Average order value: **~59.76**
* Purchase patterns consistent across age groups
* Seasonal variation impacts revenue

---

### 📊 Revenue Insights

* Total revenue: **~233K**
* Clothing and accessories dominate sales

---

### 📊 Customer Funnel Analysis

* Identified drop-offs between total and repeat customers
* Highlights need for retention strategies

---

### 📊 Spending Behavior

* High-value customers spend significantly more
* Low-value customers show conversion potential

---

## 💰 Business Impact

* Identified **high-value customers driving majority revenue**
* Highlighted **retention gaps using funnel analysis**
* Discovered **upsell opportunities across customer segments**
* Provided **category-level revenue insights for strategic focus**

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Focus on retaining high-value customers
* Convert low-value customers using targeted offers
* Optimize pricing and promotions for better ROI
* Improve customer experience to increase repeat purchases
* Use segmentation for personalized marketing
